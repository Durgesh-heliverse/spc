FAQ

SAFe® SPC 6.0 Certification FAQs

What Is A SAFE® Practice Consultant (SPC) Certification? ^

The SAFe® SPC 6.0 certification equips professionals with enterprise-ready frameworks, practical tools, and actionable strategies to lead large-scale Agile transformations and deliver measurable business impact across organizations.

Who should enroll in this course? ^

The course is ideal for Program & Project Managers, Product Managers, Business Analysts, Executives, Portfolio Managers, System Architects, Directors, Solution Architects, Agile Coaches, Development QA, and Process Leads aiming to lead enterprise-scale Agile initiatives.

What are the prerequisites for SPC 6.0 certification? ^

While there is no mandatory prior experience, it is recommended to have:

  • 5+ years in software development, testing, business analysis, product management, or project management
  • 3+ years of practical experience working in Agile environments
  • One or more relevant Agile certifications (Scrum Master, PMI-ACP, or equivalent)

What will I learn in this course? ^

You will learn:

  • Master SAFe® principles and practices
  • Lead Agile transformations across teams, programs, and enterprises
  • Coach leaders, teams, and stakeholders in Lean-Agile adoption
  • Design and implement Agile Release Trains (ARTs)
  • Align business goals with Agile execution
  • Facilitate SAFe® workshops and training sessions effectively
  • Drive continuous improvement and measurable outcomes

    • How is the SAFe® Practice Consultant (SPC 6.0) certification delivered? ^

    The four-day certification program is expert-led and highly interactive, featuring live Q&A sessions, hands-on simulations, roleplays, real-world case studies, and collaborative exercises to ensure practical learning and application.

    What are the benefits of getting SPC 6.0 certified? ^

    The benefits of getting SPC 6.0 certified include:

    • Global recognition as a SAFe® leader
    • Higher salary potential and career growth opportunities
    • Advanced Agile leadership and implementation skills
    • Ability to lead enterprise-scale Agile transformations
    • Access to a global network of certified SAFe® professionals

    Is the course suitable for beginners? ^

    Yes, the course is suitable for beginners. Even if you are new to SAFe®, the course provides the tools, mindset, and confidence to guide teams, influence change, and accelerate organizational agility.

    How do I earn the SPC 6.0 certification? ^

    To become a Certified SAFe® Practice Consultant, participants must:

    • Complete the four-day training facilitated by SAFe® Program Consultants
    • Pass the post-training assessment with a minimum score of 80%
    • Receive the official SAFe® SPC 6.0 credential from Scaled Agile, Inc.

    Can SAFe® Practice Consultant certification help me in my career globally? ^

    Absolutely. The SAFe® SPC 6.0 credential is recognized worldwide, enabling professionals to access leadership, consulting, and Agile transformation roles across industries and geographies.
